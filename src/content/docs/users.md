# Users & Roles

Hackley Clubz is used by several distinct groups, each with its own permissions
and goals.

## Students

The primary users of the platform. Students:

- Discover and browse clubs in the directory.
- Request to join clubs they are interested in.
- Read announcements and participate in club chat.
- Manage their own memberships from their profile.

## Faculty Advisors

Each club has one or more faculty advisors. Advisors:

- Approve or decline requests from students to join their club.
- Post announcements and communicate with members.
- Maintain their club's details (description, meeting times, etc.).
- See the roster of current members.

## Administrators

Administrators oversee the platform as a whole. Admins:

- Approve new clubs before they appear in the directory.
- Manage user accounts and roles.
- Monitor activity across all clubs.
- Access reporting on participation and engagement.

## Leadership (Dean & IT Director)

Leadership consumes the platform's outputs rather than its day-to-day features:

- The **Dean** reviews participation and engagement reporting to understand
  student life.
- The **IT Director** operates and maintains the system and its integrations.

> Roles are layered: an administrator can also be an advisor for a specific club,
> and an advisor is also a regular user. Permissions are additive.
