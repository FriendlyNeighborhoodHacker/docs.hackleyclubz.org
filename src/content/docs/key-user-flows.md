# Key User Flows

These are the everyday journeys a student takes through Hackley Clubz.

## 1. Discovering a club

1. The student opens the club directory.
2. They browse or search by name, category, or interest.
3. They open a club's page to read its description, meeting times, and advisor.

## 2. Joining a club

1. From a club's page, the student taps **Request to Join**.
2. The request is sent to the club's advisor for approval.
3. The student receives a notification when they are approved.
4. The club now appears in the student's list of memberships.

## 3. Staying informed

1. Approved members see club **announcements** in their feed.
2. Members can read and post in the club **chat**.
3. Important updates trigger a notification.

## 4. Managing personal membership

1. The student opens their profile.
2. They review the clubs they belong to.
3. They can leave a club at any time.

> These flows are intentionally lightweight so students can act in just a few
> taps. Anything that affects others (like joining) routes through an advisor for
> approval.
