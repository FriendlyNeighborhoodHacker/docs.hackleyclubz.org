# Team

**Lilly Rosenthal** is the primary engineer developing hackleyclubz.org. Lilly is a rising senior at Hackley and a graduate of Kode with Klossy in programming and data analysis. She has built [Ecumenical Emergency Food Pantry](https://eefoodpantry.org/) (the web site for a food pantry in White Plains), [JudyRosenthal.com](https://www.judyrosenthal.com) (a memorial site for the artist Judy Rosenthal),  and debate team management tools at Hackley.

# Advisors

**Brian Rosenthal** is advising this project. He is a former Engineering Director and Principal Engineer at Meta (Facebook) and passionate about helping people learn how to build useful applications.
